About Us

Welcome to Ayart Studio! We are Class 8 students based in Rudrapur (Udham Singh Nagar, Uttarakhand, India) who are passionate about art and technology.

Arnav Tyagi is the lead artist with over 1 year of experience in sketching and drawing. Yugank Vishnoi is a beginner artist and our talented web designer. Together, we built this platform to share our journey.

Our Mission: Many art tutorials on the internet are too complex for beginners. Our goal is to provide simple, step-by-step drawing guides and lessons so that children and beginners can learn art easily and effectively.
