About Us

Welcome to Ayart Studio! We are Class 8 students based in Rudrapur (Udham Singh Nagar, Uttarakhand, India) who are deeply passionate about fine arts and web technology.

Arnav Tyagi is the lead traditional artist with over 1 year of experience in realistic sketching and shading. Yugank Vishnoi is an aspiring web designer and beginner artist. Together, we built this creative platform to share our learning journey.

Our Mission: Most online art tutorials are overly complicated for children and absolute beginners. Our goal is to break down complex drawing techniques into simple, step-by-step structural guides so anyone can learn how to sketch easily and confidently.
